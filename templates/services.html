Our Services

Real-time Sign Language to Text Conversion

We provide a real-time sign language to text conversion service using advanced computer vision and machine learning technologies. Our system instantly translates sign language gestures into written text.

Speech Synthesis

In addition to text conversion, our service can also synthesize the translated text into spoken language. This feature enhances communication for both the hearing-impaired and those who may not understand sign language.

User-Friendly Interface

Our platform offers a user-friendly interface, making it easy for users to record and analyze sign language gestures. The intuitive design ensures a seamless experience for all users.
